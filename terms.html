Privacy and Policies

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of the Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS). This policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you interact with our website and participate in JPCS activities.

1. Information Collection

1.1. Personal Information:
We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and academic affiliation when you register for membership.

1.2. Usage Data:
We automatically collect information about your device and usage patterns when you access our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. Use of Information

2.1. Membership Management:
We use your personal information to manage membership, process payments, and communicate with you about JPCS activities and events.

2.2. Website Improvement:
We analyze usage data to improve our website, content, and user experience based on visitor preferences and trends.

3. Data Security

3.1. Protection Measures:
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

3.2. Confidentiality:
Your personal information is kept confidential and is only accessible to authorized JPCS personnel for legitimate purposes.

4. Information Sharing

4.1. Third-Party Services:
We may use third-party services such as payment processors and email providers to facilitate membership management and communications. These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and privacy policies.

4.2. Legal Compliance:
We may disclose personal information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety of members and the public.

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1. Cookies:
We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance website functionality, personalize content, and analyze usage patterns.

5.2. Opt-Out:
You can opt-out of cookies through your browser settings, although this may limit certain features and functionality of our website.

6. Data Retention

6.1. Retention Period:
We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

7.1. Access and Correction:
You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information by contacting us at contact@jpcs.org.

7.2. Opt-Out:
You may opt-out of receiving communications from JPCS or request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

9. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be communicated to members, and continued use of our services implies acceptance of the updated policy.
