Privacy and Policies

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of the Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS). This policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you interact with our website and participate in JPCS activities.

1. Website Usage

By accessing and using the JPCS website, you agree to comply with these terms and conditions. If you do not agree with any part of these terms, please refrain from using our website.

2. Membership

2.1. Membership Eligibility:
Membership in JPCS is open to students, professionals, and alumni interested in Information Technology and related fields.

2.2. Membership Fees:
Membership fees are non-refundable and must be paid annually. Fees vary based on membership type (Student, Professional, Alumni).

2.3. Membership Benefits:
JPCS members enjoy access to exclusive events, educational resources, networking opportunities, and community service projects.

3. Code of Conduct

3.1. Respect:
Members must treat others with respect and professionalism in all interactions within JPCS activities.

3.2. Integrity:
Members are expected to uphold ethical standards and integrity in their conduct and contributions.

3.3. Compliance:
Members must comply with all JPCS policies, guidelines, and directives.

4. Intellectual Property

4.1. Content Ownership:
JPCS retains ownership of all content, materials, and resources provided on the website and during JPCS activities.

4.2. Use of Materials:
Members may use JPCS materials for personal and educational purposes only. Unauthorized distribution or commercial use is prohibited.

5. Privacy Policy

5.1. Data Collection:
JPCS collects personal information for membership and communication purposes only. Information is kept confidential and not shared with third parties without consent.

5.2. Data Security:
JPCS implements security measures to protect member data. However, members are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

6. Termination

6.1. Membership Termination:
JPCS reserves the right to terminate or suspend membership for violations of terms and conduct that are deemed detrimental to the organization.

7. Changes to Terms

JPCS may update or modify these terms and conditions as needed. Members will be notified of any changes, and continued use of JPCS services implies acceptance of the updated terms.
